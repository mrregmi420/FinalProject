Welcome to EasyJobs

“EASY JOBS” is a website where people can get information about jobs. People can get information of the jobs available in the company and apply for the vacant jobs. People can send their CV to the related company with the help of this website. This “EASY JOBS” will also help employer who are searching employee for their company or Organization. Employer can easily look the C.V of the candidates through this website and can accept or reject the candidate. This website will be the bridge between the job seeker and job giver. This website will be built mainly by using python language and Django web frame work.
